Operator Profile

Zoe Founder of ZoeLab

Engineer and manga artist. After years as a systems engineer — including months of 100+ hours of overtime — Zoe discovered TaskChute, a Japanese time-management method, and finally started leaving work on time. Today, Zoe works as a TaskChute Certified Trainer while developing apps, writing books, and creating manga.

  • —TaskChute Certified Trainer with course teaching experience
  • —Professional background as a systems engineer building and operating business systems
  • —Serialized the manga column "SE Joshi no Nichijo" (30+ installments) on Da Vinci Web, a KADOKAWA media outlet
  • —Author of seven e-books on IT, productivity, and manga techniques — two published commercially by the Japanese publisher Kimpusha
  • —Extensive track record of LINE stickers, four-panel comics, and explanatory illustrations
